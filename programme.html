Academic Program

Computer Engineering — CNAM (RNCP level 7)

Home Academic Program

General Overview

  • Degree: Computer Engineering (RNCP 39126 / Level 7), accredited by CNAM.
  • Duration: 3 years — 1800 hours.
  • Work-study: 1 week in company / 1 week in training center (on-site + 25% remote).
  • Skills blocks: Design/Modeling, IT Management, Deployment/Maintenance, Testing/Integration, Software Architecture & Cybersecurity, Data/BI, Networks & Systems, Automation…

For the full program, see the official ITII Picardie website.

Program by Semester

Semester 5

  • Management, project & communication: English 5, Engineer communication, Project workshops & work-study 5, Industrial organization.
  • Engineering sciences & techniques: Mathematical tools, Electrical systems, Mechanical systems.
  • Specialization: Programming – Tools, Programming – Methods.

Semester 6

  • Management, project & communication: English 6, Project workshops & work-study 6, Sustainable development.
  • Engineering sciences & techniques: Digital filtering, Electronic systems.
  • Specialization: Design & development of an application, Analysis, Object-oriented programming, Introduction to mobile development.

Semester 7

  • Management, project & communication: English 7, Project workshops & work-study 7, Project management.
  • Engineering sciences & techniques: Artificial intelligence, Image processing.
  • Specialization: Computer component architecture, Networks & Telecom I (infrastructures).

Semester 8

  • Management: Oral communication for engineers, Business organization & management / cost estimation & calls for tenders.
  • Engineering sciences & techniques: Automation, Industrial local networks, IoT.
  • Specialization: Research initiation, Databases & Information Systems, Networks & Telecom II, Cryptography, Data & Operations Research.

Semester 9

  • Management: Project workshops & work-study 9, Management & ethics / lead & coordinate, Economic intelligence, Knowledge management & regulatory monitoring, HSE & industrial risks.
  • Specialization: Networks & Telecom III, Automation – neural networks, Java programming, PHP development.

Company activities: projects + final thesis with defense.

Career Opportunities

  • Computer Engineer, IT Manager, IT Project Manager, Head of IT Department.
